Welcome to our AR page!

COVID has put strain on a lot of people on a lot of different ways. While on other parts of our website you can donate to make a difference or even receive donations here you can have fun and relax!

We have a couple different models here, each with a different purpose. You can choose to display a 3D coronavirus or some lungs, for reference (not medical) to learn about the basic structure of the virus or how it affects different regions of the lungs. Alternatively, you could display a cute bison or a pretty fox for some silent companionship; the Earth and skyscraper are also avalible, to fill up an empty room and just have something to look at!

Simply click on the preview image and it will bring you to a QR code you can scan with your mobile app, then BOOM! You'll have something special sitting in your living room!
